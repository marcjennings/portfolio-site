Project Goals

I have no deadlines for this course but would like to complete it as quickly as I can, hopefully within 3 to 4 months. I plan to spend 6 to 8 hours a day doing the course but this could change due to work commitments.

Course Goals

My learning goals for this course would be to gain an understanding of web development, be able to design and build websites and then get into UI design. I'm open to the idea of staying as a freelancer or getting hired as a entry level devoloper in a full time role, I just thought this course would be useful as most design jobs now require some knowledge of HTML and CSS. I'm also interested in learning about responsive sites, apps, HTML email design and anything else I can pick up along the way.
